About Us


Blue Ocean Angels is a Melbourne-based Virtual Assistant recruitment firm dedicated to connecting Australian businesses with high-quality, cost-effective virtual support. We specialise in matching companies with skilled, university-educated professionals from rural communities in South East Asia, empowering both businesses and underserved regions. With flexible tiered services—ranging from entry-level support to executive virtual management—Blue Ocean Angels provides tailored assistance to streamline operations, enhance productivity, and drive growth, all at affordable rates.
